Pangrams 🌸

    Common Words

      How to Play

      • Create words using the letters in the flower.
      • Every word must include the centre letter.
      • Words must be at least 4 letters long.
      • Letters can be reused.
      • A Bloom word uses all 7 letters — bonus 7 points!

      Scoring

      • Each word scores its letter count (e.g. 4 letters = 4 pts, 6 letters = 6 pts)
      • Bloom = word score + 7 bonus
      • Bonus words: rare bonus words still count for points, but you don't need them to reach Garden Master!

      Ranks

      Earn a percentage of the common words' total score to rank up:

      • 🌱 Seedling — 0%
      • 🌿 Sprout — 10%
      • 🌼 Bud — 20%
      • 🌸 Bloom — 35%
      • 🌺 Petal — 50%
      • 🌻 Sunflower — 70%
      • 💐 Bouquet — 90%
      • 🌟 Garden Master — 100%
