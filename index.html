How to Play

  • Create words using the letters in the flower.
  • Every word must include the centre letter.
  • Words must be at least 4 letters long.
  • Letters can be reused.
  • A Bloom word uses all 7 letters — bonus 7 points!

Scoring

  • 4 letters = 1 point
  • 5 letters = 5 points
  • 6+ letters = 1 point per letter
  • Bloom = word score + 7 bonus
